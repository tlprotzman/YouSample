Instructions

  1. Select a video from YouTube
  2. Using this website, paste the youtube link, make sure mp4 is the selected format, and click start
  3. Do Not Click Download!!!
  4. Click New Sample to create a new sample bank.
  5. Right click on the download link once the conversion is done, and paste this link into the sample bank you wish to load it in
  6. Once samples are loaded, switch to the sequencer tab and create a pattern of the desired time signature and length.

Best Practices

  1. Set up all your samples first! Once the sample grid is created, it cannot be changed
